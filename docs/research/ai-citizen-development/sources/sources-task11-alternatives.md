## Notes

- No platform evaluated holds a FERPA-specific certification because no such certification exists. FERPA compliance is achieved through contractual controls, data handling practices, and platform security features rather than a single certification.
- Pricing estimates are based on publicly available information and may differ from negotiated institutional rates. Education discounts (Retool, ServiceNow via Internet2) can significantly reduce costs.
- Searches conducted March 2026. Platform features and pricing may have changed since research was conducted.
- ServiceNow pricing details are not publicly available; estimates are based on community discussions and third-party analysis.
- Lovable's $330M Series B / $6.6B valuation data comes from the market landscape draft (03-market-landscape.md), which sourced it from December 2025 reporting.
