## Evidence Quality Assessment

The strongest evidence gap in this research is the near-total reliance on vendor customer stories (primarily Microsoft) for university case studies. No independent, peer-reviewed study of citizen development programs in higher education was found. The enterprise cases (Deutsche Bahn, H&M, Medtronic) have more detailed governance documentation but come from different organizational contexts.

Ron should assess whether institutional access to the full Forrester TEI study and EDUCAUSE AI Landscape Study would strengthen the briefing's evidentiary foundation.
