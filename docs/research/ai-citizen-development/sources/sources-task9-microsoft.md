## Research Gaps

1. **Limited Copilot Studio-specific higher education case studies.** Most education deployments documented are Microsoft 365 Copilot (the assistant), not Copilot Studio (the agent-building platform). Copilot Studio citizen development in higher education is still early-stage.

2. **No independent FERPA audit of Power Platform citizen development.** Microsoft's FERPA posture is documented, but no independent assessment of FERPA compliance specifically in a citizen development context was found.

3. **Limited quantitative data on citizen development failure rates.** While qualitative accounts of Power Platform citizen development failures are abundant, rigorous quantitative studies on failure rates, maintenance costs, and long-term TCO in higher education are scarce.

4. **Gartner Peer Insights detailed ratings.** Full Gartner ratings for Copilot Studio were behind a paywall and could not be accessed for this research.
