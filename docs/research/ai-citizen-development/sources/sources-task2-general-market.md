## Notes on Evidence Quality

- Market size estimates vary significantly across analyst firms ($4B to $6.5B for no-code AI alone in 2025). This reflects differing scope definitions rather than contradictory data.
- Shadow AI statistics come from multiple independent sources and are broadly consistent (60-80% unauthorized usage range), lending confidence to the trend even if exact figures vary.
- Gartner's "2,500% increase in software defects" prediction is dramatic and should be cited with appropriate context -- it specifically refers to prompt-to-app approaches, not all citizen development.
- Several sources are vendor blogs aggregating analyst data. Where possible, primary analyst sources have been cross-referenced, but some Gartner/Forrester full reports are paywalled and only summaries are available.
