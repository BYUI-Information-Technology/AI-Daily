## Evidence Gaps Noted

- **Citizen development in higher education:** No EDUCAUSE or higher-ed-specific research directly addresses "citizen development" as a named strategy. This concept is being imported from enterprise IT literature. The term does not appear in EDUCAUSE publications.
- **BYU-Idaho peer institutions:** No published AI governance data was found for direct peer institutions (religiously affiliated, teaching-focused, ~50,000 students). Georgia Gwinnett College is the closest peer with a published policy.
- **Quantified shadow AI impact:** No higher-ed-specific study quantifies the cost or compliance impact of shadow AI. Available data focuses on prevalence rather than consequences.
- **Low-code/no-code adoption in higher ed:** Beyond the UC Tech 2025 conference session, no substantive research was found on university adoption of low-code/no-code platforms for staff-built solutions.
